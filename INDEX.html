    Our Journey: A Student's Venture into Sustainable Solutions from LPU

    From Classroom to Community. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a platform that connects people with a shared goal of reducing plastic waste. We started by conducting extensive research, analyzing the plastic waste cycle, and identifying the key challenges faced by individuals and communities. With a deep understanding of the problem, we set out to develop a comprehensive solution

    We designed a user-friendly website that facilitates the collection, segregation, and sale of recyclable plastics. By leveraging technology, we aimed to streamline the process and make it accessible to everyone. Along the way, we faced numerous challenges. From technical hurdles to logistical complexities, we persevered, learning from each setback. We sought guidance from mentors, industry experts, and environmental organizations to refine our approach and ensure its viability.
